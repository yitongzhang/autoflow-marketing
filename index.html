01 - Real nice lines

Your beautiful designs deserve beautiful lines

Autoflow lines always choose the shortest path and bends only when necessary so your canvas can remain uncluttered.

02 - Avoids obstacles

Smart lines that know where to go

Autoflow will smartly avoid other objects on the canvas so lines never draw over your designs.

03 - Custom styles

Line styles for every use case

Happy paths, sad paths, conditonals, edge cases, taps, swipes, force touch — Autoflow provides a line style to illustrate a wide range of relationships.
